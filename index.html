Who We Are

CCExtractor is a fast closed captions extractor for MPEG and H264 files. It supports DVD, HDTV transport streams, Replay TV. You can use this to create .srt (subtitles) files for your TV captures, have transcripts so you can edit subtitles, etc.

CCExtractor has been a part of Google Code-In and Google Summer of Code for many year. This is the official site for the students of Google Code-In.

How to rock GCI!

If you’ve come searching for the perfect recipe for success, you’re at the right place. We, from CCExtractor, are gonna tell you how you can highly increase your chances of winning the Grand Prize ;)

The most important thing is to keep in touch with the open source community. Follow our GitHub page, check us out on Slack, folow our mailing lists, and participate in our IRC channels. Keep contributing. And help others. This is essentially the mantra of open source.

Good Luck!
